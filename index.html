The Startup Internship Program (SIP) is a flagship initiative by E-Cell, NIT Trichy, designed to connect emerging startups with the student community of NITT by facilitating internship opportunities.
Started in the year 2009, to promote the spirit of Start-ups and promoting a sense of entrepreneurship among the students, Start-up Internship Program aims at promoting interest in entrepreneurship among students of NIT-T by giving them the opportunity to intern in Start-up companies.
